About the Author

I’m Jason Lee—also known as Galactic Monk. I'm the author of Victim, Survivor, Warrior, the founder of RABBIT & TRAIL, 1/2 of Billion Years Old, and a chaneller of just about any concept that inspires me.

My life isn’t easy, even if it may look that way. I’m an alchemist. I don’t just preach love and light—I am also the sum of what I’ve survived and transmuted: chaos, thunder, and darkness.

I am a living, diagnosed schizophrenic, a label that shuns me every day. I, myself, do not feel I am one, although my psychiatrist disagrees. Nevertheless, I am always building my own universe, believing that what I create will one day overshadow the stigma this diagnosis has introduced into my life. As someone who shoots for the stars, I do not have the luxury of shouting "disability" as an excuse.

I write books, have a passion for travel photography, and actively speak exactly what’s on my mind on X (formerly Twitter), without caring who gets offended. I upgrade my mind, body, and spirit daily through the disciplines of Mixed Martial Arts and Hot Yoga. I am the black sheep who could never quite fit in, regardless of how much I tried.

If you’re looking for a guy to tell you what you want to hear instead of what you need to hear, look elsewhere. If you want authenticity, a no-nonsense mindset, and a warrior who never stops fighting—no matter how adverse life gets—look no further. I'm the one you've been looking for all along.
